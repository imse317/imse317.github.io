Poisson distribution

A call center receives an average of $\lambda$ (e.g., 4) independent calls per minute.

How likely to receive exact $k$ calls during any minute?

$\lambda=$


$$ p(k) = \begin{cases} \frac{\lambda^k e^{-\lambda}}{k!}, & \text{if $k = 0, 1, \cdots$,} \\ 0, & \text{otherwise.} \end{cases} \\[16pt] \text{where $\lambda$ is the average event rate.} $$
